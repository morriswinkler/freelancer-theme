About

I am a Soft- and Hardware developer from Berlin in Germany with more than ten years of experience. Equipped with practical knowledge in the areas of systems and application programming, robotics, and computer-aided manufacturing.

During the last four years, I gave countless lectures and workshops on 3D Printing, 3D or CAD modeling, and microcontroller programming. My personal record is 100+ 3D Printer building classes which in its early state inspired the development of the i3 Berlin.

So far my work allowed me to come across various aspects of product and service design. If I would distil all of it down to the quintessence, I probably end up with something like 'Simplicity is key'. But the moment that I came up with this little insight, I needed to admit that 'Simplicity is difficult'.

I am well trained in operating various types of digital fabrication equipment, in particular 3D Printers, Laser Cutters, and CNC Machines. More than handling I have written software for most of these machines and I am very familiar with the concepts and mechanisms that drive them.
